## Topics I Speak On

<div class="topics-grid">

  <div class="topic-card">
    <h4>🏗️ Building Data Governance From Zero</h4>
    <p>What it actually takes to build a data governance programme in an African organisation — the politics, the tools, the quick wins, and the long game.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="topic-card">
    <h4>🤖 AI Safety for African Contexts</h4>
    <p>Why AI safety is not just a Silicon Valley concern — and what African organisations and regulators need to be asking right now.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="topic-card">
    <h4>🔒 The Kenya Data Protection Act: What It Really Requires</h4>
    <p>A practical walkthrough of DPA 2019 compliance for organisations across sectors — what's required, what's commonly missed, and how to prioritise.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="topic-card">
    <h4>👩🏾‍💻 Women in Data: The Invisible Labour Problem</h4>
    <p>On the pattern of African women doing senior technical work at junior titles — and what organisations, institutions, and women themselves can do about it.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="topic-card">
    <h4>📊 Data-Driven Decision Making for African Organisations</h4>
    <p>How to build the evidence infrastructure — from data quality to dashboards — that makes strategy defensible and accountability real.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="topic-card">
    <h4>🌍 Responsible AI for Marginalised Communities</h4>
    <p>The specific risks that AI poses to women, rural populations, and the economically marginalised in Africa — and what responsible deployment looks like.</p>
  </div>
